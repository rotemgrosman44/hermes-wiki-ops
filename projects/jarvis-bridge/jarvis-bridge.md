# Jarvis Bridge

The bridge is the approved handoff layer between Jarvis and Hermes.

## Allowed Shape
- Inbound bundle from Jarvis.
- Hermes processing and bounded synthesis.
- Return bundle back to Jarvis.

## Disallowed Shape
- Direct canon writes.
- Live authority bypass.
- Vault sharing as a source of truth.
